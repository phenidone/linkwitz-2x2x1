

Invert mixer:
V1/R1+V2/R1+Vo/R2 = 0
Vo=-R3(V1/R1+V2/R1)


Sallen-Key Butterworth, low-pass

w^2=1/R.R.C1.C2
with C2=2C1, 
f=1/(pi.2^1.5*R*C1)
f=15Hz, C1=1e-6, R=7.5k, 200n & 39k.

w=1/sqrt(R.R.C1.C2)

z.w = (R.C2+R.C2+R.C1-A*R.C1) / (2*R.R.C1.C2)

with C1=C2, w=1/RC
z.w = (3*R.C-A*R.C) / (2*R^2*C^2)
z= (3*R*C-A*R*C) / 2RC
z = (3 - A) / 2

with C1=2*C2=C, w=1/RCsqrt2
z.w = (4*RC - 2*ARC) / (4*R^2*C^2)
z = sqrt2*(4 - 2A) / 4
A=1
z = sqrt2*2/4 = 0.707 w00t
